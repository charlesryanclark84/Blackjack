# Project Title
Blackjack
A card game where 21 is the winning number. You play against the dealer. If you go over 21 you lose. If you don't have 21 whoever has the higher number wins. Good luck and always play and bet safely. 
Gambling issues call 1-800-522-4700 for help.

## 🚀 Features
A simple interface that shows the dealer and the players cards.
The three button on the bottom allow you to interact with the interface.
The Hit button will give the player another card.
The Stay button will stop the player's turn, then the dealer's turn will take place.
After pressing the stay button and the dealer's turn a message will appear to say who won.
The New Game button will refresh the screen so you can play again.
